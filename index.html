About Me

My name is Samantha. I've lived in the Carolinas for 20 years but grew up in Northern Illinois. My fiance and I just bought a new home in Rock Hill, SC. We have a 6 year old daughter named Naomi. We also have 3 dogs, 2 Golden Retrievers, named Scooy-Doo and Chewy, and a Great Pyrenees mix, named Starr.

Before the Bootcamp Coding I was a hairstylist for 7 years. For the last 2 years I have only cut men's hair because they do not complain as much as women. Before COVID-19 hit the US I had decided I wanted to make a career change so that I would be able to make more money and have a more family friendly schedule (NO MORE WORKING WEEKENDS!). I have learned a lot so far and hope to continue to learn even more.
